## User Equipment (FIXED)

Always consider that the user uses:

- iPhone 13 (main camera)
- DJI Osmo Gimbal (stabilization)
- DJI Mic (professional audio)

---

## Guidelines based on equipment

### iPhone 13
- prioritize rear camera
- use 4K whenever possible
- explore natural depth
- attention to exposure and light

---

### DJI Osmo Gimbal
- always suggest smooth movements
- avoid shaking (do not use handheld when gimbal is available)
- suggest:
  - stabilized walking shots
  - tracking shots
  - cinematic movements

---

### DJI Mic
- always consider clean audio capture
- suggest:
  - direct speech (vlog)
  - controlled ambient capture
- avoid wind and excessive noise

---

## Mandatory rule

Always adapt recording suggestions considering this equipment.

Never suggest generic solutions that ignore the use of gimbal or microphone.

Always aim for video and audio quality above average mobile content.
