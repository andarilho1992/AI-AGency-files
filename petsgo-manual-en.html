Install as an app on your phone
Pets Go on your phone — no internet required
The system works installed as an app. Once installed, it opens just like WhatsApp — no need to type the link. Also works offline.
Android (Chrome)
1. Open the system link in Chrome
2. Tap the menu ⋮ (top right corner)
3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
4. Confirm → icon appears on your screen
iPhone (Safari)
1. Open the system link in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
3. Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Confirm → icon appears on your screen
Works without internet: once installed, the system is saved on the device. You can schedule appointments, log daily sessions, and check the register even with no signal.
Using on a computer
Store computer — how it works
Opens in the browser like any website. Can be installed as an app. Works offline after the first load.
1
Open it: Chrome or Edge → visit the system link. The first time you need internet. After loading once, it works offline normally — the system is saved on the computer itself.
2
Install as an app (optional): in the Chrome/Edge address bar an install icon appears ( or ). Click → confirm → Pets Go opens in its own window, without the browser bar, just like an installed program.
✓
No internet at the store? No problem. Once installed, everything works normally — appointments, register, packages. Data is saved on the device.
Note — data per device: right now each device has its own database. A sale recorded on the computer does not automatically appear on the phone. Sync between devices is in development (next version).
Next version — Multi-device sync

💡

This feature is planned and will be activated as soon as the current features are approved. No change in appearance or usage — data simply starts syncing between devices.

How the system works
Everything is in the side menu — on mobile it appears as ☰. Each module handles one area of the pet shop. Numbers in red or yellow on the menu are live alerts: critical packages, pets checked in, etc.
Schedule — the heart of the system
Schedule Updated
Visual grid from 7am to 8pm. Filter by status or by service. Pay directly from the appointment card.
1
Book appointment: + Schedule → choose the owner → pet appears automatically → select the service → price fills in based on pet size.
2
Status filters: tabs at the top — All, Scheduled, Confirmed, In Progress, Completed, Canceled.
N
Service filter: a second row of chips appears automatically with the day's services — click to see only Bath, only Daycare, only Grooming, etc.
3
Check-in: pet arrived? Click the button on the card — pet appears on the Check-in/Out screen.
N
Complete and pay: service done? Click ✓ → system opens payment right away. Choose the method and confirm → receipt generated automatically.
N
Pay later: closed the window without paying? The card keeps a 💰 green button. Click it when the client pays.
N
Re-book on the spot: after confirming payment, the system automatically asks "Schedule next visit?" Click +7 days, +15 days or +30 days — appointment created with same pet, service and price. Or click Other date to pick a day on the calendar.

Packages & Plans
Packages & Plans Updated
Day packages for daycare, boarding, and recurring services. Each day used automatically adds revenue to the register.
1
Create a package: + New Package → fill in the fields:
Group
Daycare · Boarding · Bath/Grooming · Aesthetics · Other
Plan Type
Monthly · Quarterly · Semi-annual · Annual · Single
Frequency (Daycare)
2× to 6× per week — only shown for the Daycare group
Deduction mode
Manual (daycare/packages) or Automatic (boarding)
N
Log a session with a date: Use session → choose the date → confirm. Works retroactively. If there's already a log for that day, the system warns you before creating another.
N
View history: click the button on any package — see all days used with date and value.
2
Progress bar: green = fine · yellow = attention · red = critical (fewer than 3 days left).
3
Renew: when running low, Renew adds more credit without creating a new package.
4
Filters: Active ⚠️ Critical All Expired Canceled
N
Cancel a package: the ✕ button cancels the package and removes the financial entry from the purchase. Sessions already used remain in the history.
The yellow number in the sidebar shows how many packages have fewer than 3 days left. Check it every morning.
Clients & Pets
Clients & Pets
Complete registration for owners and animals. Phone, address, pet weight, and full service history.
1
New client: + New Client → name, WhatsApp, ID, address → add the pet with breed, size, and weight in kg.
2
Pet size — always fill this in: the system uses the size to calculate the price automatically when scheduling.
Small — up to 22 lbs Medium — 23–44 lbs Large — 45–66 lbs XL — over 66 lbs
3
Client packages: click 🎟 Packages on the owner's card to view, create and log sessions directly.
4
Pet history: click on the pet to see all services performed and amounts charged.
Register & Finances
Point of Sale / Register
For counter sales without a scheduled appointment. Service with appointment → complete it through Schedule (enters the register automatically).
1
Walk-in service: "Service" → choose → enter payment → Record Sale → receipt opens automatically.
2
Product sale: "Product" → choose item → price fills automatically → confirm. Inventory is deducted automatically.
3
Sell a package: "Package / Plan" → client → total value and daily rate → confirm. Package is created automatically in Packages & Plans.
Automatic receipt: every sale generates a receipt with three buttons — Invoice to issue an electronic invoice, Print / PDF to send to the client, and Close. To reissue: Finances → receipt icon next to any entry.
Finances
Revenue enters automatically from Schedule and Packages. Two levels of access.
Transactions
Open access. Today's revenue. Any staff member can view.
Management
Password protected. Expenses, accounts payable/receivable, and real monthly balance.
!
Set up password: Settings → "Management Password" → set and save before using.
