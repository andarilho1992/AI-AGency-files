# Um Forasteiro — Content Pillars

## Pillar 1: Process
How I build systems. Educational, shows expertise.
Example: "How I built a full CRM in 3 days using Claude Code"

## Pillar 2: Life
Nomad reality, Chiang Mai, the contrast between work and lifestyle.
Example: "Working from a café in Thailand while my clients sleep in Brazil"

## Pillar 3: Results
Client wins, things I shipped, real numbers.
Example: "First paying client. Here's what I built and what it cost me."

## Pillar 4: Behind the Scenes
Tools, failures, real numbers, honest moments.
Example: "I spent 6 hours debugging one line. Here's what I learned."

## Voice Rules
- Honest and raw — no motivational language
- Specific numbers over vague claims
- Solo nomad narrative (even though traveling with partner — brand is solo)
- Contrast: high technology + tight budget + nomadism
- Never: "hustle", "grind", "crush it", "game changer"
